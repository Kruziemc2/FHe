🌌 廣義相對論核心公式 | Core Equations of General Relativity

廣義相對論（GR）描述時空的彎曲及其與能量的相互作用。核心方程是愛因斯坦場方程： General Relativity (GR) describes the curvature of spacetime and its interaction with energy. The core equation is Einstein's field equation:

\[ R_{\mu\nu} - \frac{1}{2} g_{\mu\nu} R + \Lambda g_{\mu\nu} = \frac{8\pi G}{c^4} T_{\mu\nu} \]
  • \( R_{\mu\nu} \)：里奇曲率張量 | Ricci curvature tensor
  • \( g_{\mu\nu} \)：度規張量 | Metric tensor
  • \( R \)：標量曲率 | Scalar curvature
  • \( \Lambda \)：宇宙常數 | Cosmological constant
  • \( T_{\mu\nu} \)：能量動量張量 | Energy-momentum tensor

🌀 黑洞解 & 時空測地線 | Black Hole Solutions & Geodesics

史瓦西解（Schwarzschild Metric）：

\[ ds^2 = -\left( 1 - \frac{2GM}{c^2r} \right) c^2 dt^2 + \frac{dr^2}{1 - \frac{2GM}{c^2r}} + r^2 d\Omega^2 \]

測試粒子的測地線方程 | Geodesic equation for test particles:

\[ \frac{d^2x^\mu}{d\tau^2} + \Gamma^\mu_{\alpha\beta} \frac{dx^\alpha}{d\tau} \frac{dx^\beta}{d\tau} = 0 \]

🔢 數學推導與計算 | Mathematical Derivations & Computations

1️⃣ **事件視界半徑 | Schwarzschild Radius**

\[ r_s = \frac{2GM}{c^2} \]

2️⃣ **引力紅移 | Gravitational Redshift**

\[ z = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - \frac{2GM}{c^2 r}}} - 1 \]
