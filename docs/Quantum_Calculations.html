🧬 量子力學核心公式 | Core Equations of Quantum Mechanics

量子力學描述微觀世界的物理現象，其核心方程為薛丁格方程： Quantum mechanics describes the physical phenomena of the microscopic world. The core equation is the Schrödinger equation:

\[ i\hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \Psi (r,t) = \hat{H} \Psi (r,t) \]
  • \( \Psi(r,t) \)：波函數 | Wavefunction
  • \( \hbar \)：約化普朗克常數 | Reduced Planck's constant
  • \( \hat{H} \)：哈密頓算符 | Hamiltonian operator
