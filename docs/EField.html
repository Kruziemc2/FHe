質能方程 | Mass-Energy Equation

📌 摘要 | Abstract

質能等價方程揭示了能量與質量之間的關係，並成為現代物理學的基石。 此理論由愛因斯坦於 1905 年提出，並應用於核能、粒子物理與宇宙學等領域。

The mass-energy equivalence equation reveals the fundamental relationship between energy and mass. Proposed by Einstein in 1905, this theory has become a cornerstone of modern physics, influencing nuclear energy, particle physics, and cosmology.

📐 公式 | Equation

質能等價公式：

$$ E = mc^2 $$

The mass-energy equivalence equation:

$$ E = mc^2 $$

🔬 物理現象與應用 | Physical Phenomena & Applications

這一方程在核裂變與核融合中極為重要，例如原子彈和氫彈的能量釋放機制。 在宇宙學中，它解釋了如何將質量轉換為輻射與能量，並影響宇宙膨脹。

This equation is crucial in nuclear fission and fusion, such as the energy release mechanism in atomic bombs and hydrogen bombs. In cosmology, it explains how mass converts into radiation and energy, influencing cosmic expansion.

📐 推導過程 | Derivation Process

By relativistic mechanics, we derive this equation from four-momentum:

$$ E^2 - (pc)^2 = (mc^2)^2 $$

🧪 數據與模擬 | Data & Simulations

在粒子加速器實驗中，科學家透過高能碰撞測試質能等價，驗證了高能質子與電子的質量變化與能量釋放。

In particle accelerator experiments, scientists test mass-energy equivalence through high-energy collisions, confirming mass variations and energy release in high-energy protons and electrons.

📜 結論 | Conclusion

質能等價為現代物理奠定基礎，影響粒子物理、核能技術與宇宙學等領域。 進一步研究可探索量子引力如何影響這一公式的適用性。

Mass-energy equivalence is a cornerstone of modern physics, impacting particle physics, nuclear energy, and cosmology. Further research may explore how quantum gravity influences this equation's applicability.
